About

Crystal Butterfly is an HTML5 game.

The goal of this game is to get the highest score.

You have match minimum 3 items vertically or horizontally to win points.

When you arrive to a specific amount of points your level is increased. You win more points and time decrease faster.

There are 3 modes of difficulty.

Each week the score board will be reveiwed and the highest scorer will win a 25% discount. Be sure to fill out our form so we can varify your win.
